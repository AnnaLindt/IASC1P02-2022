MEDIA

We are the public library in Königheim.

We are 13 volunteers and 2000 pieces of media.

Our media stock offers books and magazines for young and old, from picture books to children's books and comics as well as children's CDs to novels, biographies, crime novels, non-fiction, audio books and local history novels. In order to always be up to date, we add new media several times a year and are always happy to receive book suggestions from our readers. Of course, books can also be ordered from us at any time.

We are very involved in our community and participate in events with smaller exhibitions and activities related to reading.

EVENTS:

Reading Time

Once a month we offer the event "Reading Time" where we try to teach children between the ages of 4 and 10 the fun of reading. Learn more

Christmas Market

Every December we organize a small and cozy christmas market. Come by and look forward to lots of Christmas books, mulled wine, and homemade cookies.

Book Fair

Together with other libraries in the region, we visit the Frankfurt Book Fair every year in October.
