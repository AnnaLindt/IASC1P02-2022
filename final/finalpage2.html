READING TIME:


Once a month we offer the event "Reading Time" where we try to teach children between the ages of 4 and 10 the fun of reading.

One of our volunteers will read a interesting and new childrensbook to the children. After and during the reading process they will discuss the content of the book together. At the end of each session we normally incorporate some kind of creative task related to the book like crafting or painting.
